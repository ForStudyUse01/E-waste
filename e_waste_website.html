How E-Cycle AI Works

Our innovative platform makes e-waste recycling simple, rewarding, and transparent.

1

Scan Your E-Waste

Take a photo of your electronic device using our app or website. Our AI instantly identifies the item and provides recycling recommendations or repair options.

2

Get Personalized Recommendations

E-Cycle AI analyzes your device and recommends whether to repair, repurpose, or recycle. For repairable items, receive step-by-step repair guides or connections to local repair services.

3

Find Recycling Centers

Locate the nearest smart recycling bins or e-waste collection centers using our built-in map. Check real-time availability and operating hours.

4

Deposit & Earn Rewards

Deposit your e-waste at any of our smart bins. Scan the QR code to link the transaction to your account and earn eco-points, which can be redeemed for rewards.

5

Track Your Impact

Monitor your environmental contribution through our blockchain-verified tracking system. See exactly how your e-waste is processed and the positive impact you've made.
